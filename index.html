Using this on multiple devices: without a token, edits made here stay only in this browser. With a token, adding or selling a position writes directly to positions.json in your repo — so any device with the same repo + token entered stays in sync. Create one at github.com → Settings → Developer settings → Fine-grained tokens, scoped to just this repo with read/write access to "Contents" and "Actions". Treat it like a password — anyone with it can edit that repo.
How this works: a position is long-term once held 365+ days, which usually taxes gains at the lower long-term rate. This tool compares your after-tax profit at today's price against your threshold, and tells you when waiting for long-term status is worth more than selling now. This is a planning aid, not tax or investment advice; confirm figures with a tax professional before filing.
Always-on monitoring: this page only runs while it's open. Enter that repo above and hit Sync to pull in its latest findings — see the included README for one-time setup.

Educational tool only — not financial, investment, or tax advice. Tax calculations use flat rates you supply and do not account for AMT, state taxes, wash-sale rules, net investment income tax, or your full income picture. Capital-loss offsets, cost-basis methods (FIFO/LIFO/specific-lot), and wash sales are not modeled. Live and automated prices come from third-party data and may be delayed or occasionally unavailable. The "safe stock candidates" panel is a mechanical screen (recent price volatility and trend vs. a fixed list of large-cap tickers) — it is not personalized, not a guarantee of safety, and not a recommendation. It never buys or sells anything on your behalf; every purchase or sale decision, and its timing, is entirely yours. Verify all figures with a licensed tax professional or financial advisor before making decisions.
